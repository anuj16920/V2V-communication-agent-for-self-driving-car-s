  Agents         : 8 V2V pairs  (n=8 validation run)
  Subchannels    : 4
  Power levels   : 4  (0.1W, 0.3W, 0.6W, 1.0W)
  Action space   : 16  (4 channels × 4 power)
  Episode length : 50 steps
  Total episodes : 5000 per algorithm

  QMIX Hyperparameters:
    d_model=256, n_heads=8, n_layers=3, d_ff=512
    mixing_embed_dim=64, batch_size=256, tau=0.005
    lr=1e-4, epsilon_steps=120,000, grad_clip=10.0
    n_steps=3 (n-step returns), PER capacity=200,000

  MAPPO Hyperparameters:
    d_model=256, lr_actor=3e-5, lr_critic=1e-4
    clip_eps=0.1, entropy_coef=0.02, entropy_min=0.001
    epochs=4, minibatch=32, grad_clip=0.5
    Orthogonal initialization, value clipping, cosine LR annealing

  Curriculum (3 stages):
    Stage 1 — Collision Avoidance  (advances: rolling collision < 0.28, min 600 eps)
    Stage 2 — PDR Optimization     (advances: rolling PDR > 0.40, min 600 eps)
    Stage 3 — Full Multi-Objective (remaining episodes)
    Buffer cleared + LR warm-restart on each transition

  State: 32-dim per agent (28 base + 4 Manhattan: LoS ratio, intersection dist,
         road orientation, heading)
  Manhattan channel model: LoS exponent=2.1, NLoS exponent=3.8, NLOS_FACTOR=-15dB

  KEY FINDINGS & ANALYSIS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  1. MAPPO outperforms QMIX significantly in Manhattan scenario.
     Root cause: QMIX monotonicity constraint prevents it from learning
     "all agents on same channel is bad" when correlated CSI on same street
     causes all individual Q-values to prefer the same channel.
     MAPPO's centralized critic handles this coordination problem naturally.

  2. MAPPO PDR (0.251) reaches 67% of Greedy CSI PDR (0.376).
     Greedy CSI has oracle knowledge of CSI and no coordination cost.
     MAPPO must balance channel diversity + power + QoS simultaneously.

  3. Round Robin achieves lowest collision (0.143 = theoretical minimum for
     8 vehicles / 4 channels). But PDR=0.038 — pure channel diversity with
     no power or timing optimization is insufficient for reliable delivery.
     MARL must jointly optimize channel + power, explaining the gap to CSI.

  4. QMIX coordination failure: collision peaked at 0.686 at ep 1600
     (4.8× worse than Round Robin). This is a known QMIX limitation for
     tasks requiring heterogeneous joint actions with correlated observations.
     WQMIX or QPLEX would be a stronger QMIX-family baseline.

  5. MAPPO throughput (0.607) vs Greedy CSI (0.780): the gap is primarily
     due to remaining collision (0.270). Reducing collision further would
     directly raise SINR, throughput, and PDR.

  6. Entropy collapse in late Stage 3: entropy_min=0.001 caused policy
     determinism too early. Best checkpoint at ep ~4000 (entropy=0.005)
     significantly outperforms final model (entropy=0.0018).
     FIXED in train_sota.py: entropy_min raised to 0.005.
